Boracay

I remember the time I went to Boracay I was about 6 year old at that time. I would let my mom and dad burry in the sand and we would also run around the beach any play tag. I really wanted to ride the banana boat but my parents did not let me because they told me that there were sharks in the ocean. I look at Boracay as a peaceful place humble place I even made a few friends when I was in Boracay. I was only in Boracay for a short period of time but I made the best memories.
